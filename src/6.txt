At a global level, energy represents the capacity for the functioning of the processes of all the actors. It is an indispensable resource for important issues such as the work of multinational industries, even everyday needs such as turning on electronic devices in homes or using means of transport; all the above are functions that require energy. This reflects the dependence that exists towards the energy sector, and therefore, for its use in almost all the scenarios where man develops and its social, political, economic or cultural developments; energy has become one of the main factors in issues of environmental damage. The use of different types of energies has accompanied man through his process of evolution and has allowed him to achieve objectives at every step. Currently, this resource generates the highest levels of pollution on the environment, mainly the so-called conventional energies.
